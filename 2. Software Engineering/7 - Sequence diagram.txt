### Importance of Sequence Diagrams:

- **Behavioral Visualization:** Illustrates the interaction between different components or objects in a system, showcasing the order of messages and their flow.

- **Understanding Interactions:** Provides a detailed view of how objects collaborate, communicate, and interact with each other during a specific process or scenario.

- **Design Verification:** Assists in verifying the design logic by demonstrating how objects or components interact in real-time scenarios, aiding in validation.

- **Communication Tool:** Serves as an effective communication tool among developers, designers, and stakeholders, facilitating discussions on system behavior and interactions.
